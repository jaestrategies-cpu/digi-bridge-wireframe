What is Digi-Bridge / STEAMKit Build

Finding a meaningful volunteer experience your team actually wants to do is harder than it should be.

STEAMKit Build by Digi-Bridge solves that. A local nonprofit. A fully managed experience. A story worth telling.


A STEAMKit is a hands-on learning kit filled with materials for fun, educational projects that help students explore science, technology, engineering, arts, and math at home. Your team assembles these kits while making a direct impact on students in Charlotte's underrepresented communities. Your company covers the cost of supplies. We take care of everything else.
